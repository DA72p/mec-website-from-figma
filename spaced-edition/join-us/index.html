Join MEC!

Why Join MEC:

  1. Learn essential entrepreneurship and leadership skills at our Startup School for new members. Discover other e-ship resources on campus.
  2. Get to know and work on cool projects with fellow MEC members through exciting internal events. This includes our IAP retreat, coffee chats, and more.
  3. Connect and network with alumni, other college e-ship organizations, professors, VC firms, and startups.
  4. If interested, tap into our resources to find startup career opportunities.

Our Recruitment Process: Invite Week!

You don't need to have prior "entrepreneurship experience" to join us for Invite Week. We care about your passions and future ambitions. Furthermore, Invite Week is an incredible opportunity to meet other students on campus who are interested in entrepreneurship!
