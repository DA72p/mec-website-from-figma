About Startup Career Fair

The annual spring Startup Career Fair pairs hundreds of MIT students to startup career opportunities. Our mission is to help students who are interested in entrepreneurship explore startup life while working on high-impact projects they are passionate about.

Check out our sponsorship document for more details. If interested, complete the interest form below and we will reach out to you soon! Join As A Student

Looking for career opportunities? By joining the startup fair, you will be intelligently matched to potential startup internships, full-time positions, and part-time positions. Any student interested in exploring entrepreneurship or working at a startup can apply! Fill out the interest form below to get updates for the startup fair! If you have any questions, contact our MIT Events Lead, Qudus Shittu.
