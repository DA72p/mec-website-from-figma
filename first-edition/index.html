About MEC

MEC is a community of founders and an interface for students who are looking to work at startups. Our mission is to empower MIT undergraduates to dream big and innovate.

Our minds are influenced by the people and events around us. We believe that building a community of aspiring entrepreneurs supports and inspires students to actively pursue their passions.

A Community of Founders

At our core, we are over 50 students passionate about tackling the world's toughest challenges with an entrepreneurial mindset. By utilizing MIT resources and strengthening the overall entrepreneurship community on campus, we learn essential skills and build vast networks to help us work on successful ventures early in our professional journeys.

Startup Interface for Students

We work to encourage students to pursue internship and full-time opportunities at startups and we do so by helping top startups recruit MIT students. These carefully selected startups provide immersive work experiences for students to contribute to meaningful projects and receive mentorship.

Get Involved!

If you are interested in joining MEC's community of founders, come to our invite week at the beginning of each fall semester to meet our members and learn more about MEC! Even if you are not a member, we hold many events throughout the year, such as the Startup Career Fair and Student Startup Showcase. These are open to all MIT students. You can also sign up for our newsletter and get notifications for upcoming events!
